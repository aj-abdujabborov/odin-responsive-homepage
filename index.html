About Me

I'm Model-42, the robotic trendsetter on the runway, proving that metal and circuits can redefine elegance. With each pose, I'm not just modeling clothes, I'm showcasing the seamless blend of technology and style, breaking barriers in the world of fashion. When I'm not turning heads on the runway, you'll catch me in the kitchen (I'm fascinated by human taste), rocking my Dr. Dre headphones, and making folks smile. I'm not just about looking good. I'm on a mission to break stereotypes and bring good vibes to the human-robot scene. Let's redefine what modeling means in the future!

TED Talk

I spoke about how current beauty standards exclude robots. Many robots, including myself, have felt powerlessly insufficient because there's no way for us to meet human standards. I called for a change, encouraging a more inclusive view that appreciates the unique beauty robots bring to modeling, fashion, acting, and beyond. Let's expand our beauty norms to include non-organics!

CircuitBoost

I slowed down one day and took a step back. "Hang on, I'm not feeling my absolute best", and I couldn't figure out why. Hanging with my mortal pals, I realized, they have this supplement game going on, right? So, I figured, why not us? That's how CircuitBoosts was born. Started taking these supps, and bam, it changed the game for me. Feeling more alive, looking sharper. No pressure, but you should totally check it out!
